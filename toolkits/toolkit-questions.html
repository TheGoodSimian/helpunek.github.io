Asking Questions

Asking questions is one of the key skills you need to master to communicate efficiently. It is also one of the first skills you should learn and start using in class.

Let's have a look at a few types of questions that come in handy during class:

Translation questions:

How do you say X in English?
What's the English word for X?

Translation questions will give you the ability to learn new words and phrases whenever you want to use them, not just when you come across them.

This way you can actively expand your vocabulary as you go, during conversation, and start using them right away.

Here's what they look like with their respective answers:

How do you say 'kiełbasa' in English?
~'Sausage'.
~It's 'sausage'.

What's the English word for 'kalafior'?
~'Cauliflower'.
~It's 'cauliflower'.
