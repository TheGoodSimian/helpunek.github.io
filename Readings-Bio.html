Bio / Autodescription

Zwróć uwagę, że w biogramach często używane są czasy perfektowe, a przede wszystkim present perfect simple oraz present perfect continuous. Jest tak dlatego, że biogram dotyczy tego kim ktoś jest teraz. Odniesienie do przeszłości służy wyjaśnieniu obecnego stanu rzeczy (a w tym przypadku osoby).

Photography has always been my greatest passion. I have been honing my skills behind the lens for more than a decade now. Throughout my journey, I have captured countless awe-inspiring photos, and my work has been featured in several prestigious magazines and exhibitions.

My adventures have taken me to some of the most remote corners of the globe. I have hiked through dense rainforests, having unforgettable encounters with exotic wildlife. I have also trekked across vast deserts, marveling at the endless dunes and starry skies.

In addition to photography, I have also been involved in various philanthropic projects. I have volunteered with organizations that focus on environmental conservation and community development. By working closely with these communities, I have been able to witness the positive impact of collective efforts.

Lately, I have been exploring the art of storytelling through short films. The power of visual storytelling intrigues me, and I find joy in conveying compelling narratives through my lens.
