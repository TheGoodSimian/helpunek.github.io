Proste Angedoty

Przeczytaj poniższe wypowiedzi. Być może zauważysz, że w prostych anegdotach przeważają czasy past continuous oraz past simple. Jak myślisz, dlaczego tak jest?

Last weekend, while I was baking a cake in the kitchen, it started raining outside. My favorite song was playing on the radio. Suddenly, the power went out. I found a flashlight and lit candles. Despite the darkness, the cake continued baking. I waited, savoring the sound of the rain and the distant thunder. When the cake was ready, I had a delightful slice with hot tea as the rain subsided. The power outage had added a touch of adventure to my baking.
